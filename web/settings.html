About SpenDEX

SpenDEX is a powerful personal finance application designed to help you take control of your money, track your spending, and achieve your financial goals.

With SpenDEX, you can easily manage your budgets, track expenses, analyze your spending patterns, and get insights into your financial health.

Features

  • Track income and expenses
  • Create and manage budgets
  • Visualize spending with charts and reports
  • Set and track financial goals
  • Bill reminders and due dates
  • Multi-currency support
  • Data backup and sync
